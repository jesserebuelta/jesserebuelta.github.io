Importance of our Workshop

The importance of our workshop is to reduce the trash that are thrown everywhere, and it also helps in reducing the risk of illness you might get from the wastes that aren’t disposed of properly.


Cause of our problem

Many people aren’t disposing their trash properly, most factories throw their waste at the beach or sea due to lack of proper infrastructures to throw waste in. Which causes pollution, making the community ill because of the polluted environment.


Solution/s to our Problem

One of the many possible solutions to improper waste disposal in the Philippines would be strict implementation of the RA No. 9003 since it is not being followed properly. Another one would be to build proper infrastructures to segregate our waste and appropriate gear for those who collect our waste. They deserve to be treated with respect since they get dirty for our own cleanliness. Another method we're all familiar with is CLAYGo, it is important that we observe CLAYGo so that there will be less waste buildup.
