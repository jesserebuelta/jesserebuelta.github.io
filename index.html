Team Green: A workshop on Improper Waste Disposal In The Philippines

By Group 6 Team 2


Introduction

The Green Team aims to educate our audience about improper waste disposal in the Philippines by conducting a workshop. In our workshop, we will discuss proper waste disposal, the causes and effects of improper waste disposal, and the possible solutions.
